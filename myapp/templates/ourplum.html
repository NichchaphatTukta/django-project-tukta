Marian plum is a fruit tree that has the shape of a cylinder or oval and is quite sharp. A strong taproot There are quite thick branches About 15-30 meters tall, the leaves Marian plum. Mango leaves are similar but smaller.The leaves are long and slender green leathery leaves with smooth edges.
